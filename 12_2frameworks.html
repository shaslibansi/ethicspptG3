Other Deontological Frameworks

-W. D. Ross's pluralistic deontology: Multiple prima facie duties (e.g., fidelity, justice, beneficence).


Ross’s List of Prima Facie Duties

  • Fidelity: The duty to keep promises and be truthful.
  • Reparation: The duty to make amends for past wrongs.
  • Gratitude: The duty to be thankful for the benefits others have provided.
  • Justice: The duty to promote fairness and give each their due.
  • Benevolence: The duty to help others and promote their well-being
  • Self-improvement: The duty to improve oneself intellectually and morally.
  • Non-maleficence: The duty to avoid harm to others.
